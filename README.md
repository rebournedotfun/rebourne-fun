### 💀 What is Reborne.fun?

**Reborne.fun** is a Solana resurrection protocol. Not a launchpad — a revival engine.

Memecoins don’t die. They’re abandoned. We let degens revive them through a mechanism we call **CTO** (Call-To-Own). This isn’t just a product — it’s a belief that the graveyard is full of future legends.

---

### 🧪 Features

- 🧬 **CTO (Call-To-Own):** Claim leadership of abandoned tokens
- 🪦 **Token Scanner:** Index and filter dead Solana coins
- 🧾 **On-chain Revival Logs:** Transparent, cryptographic proofs
- 🎲 **Degen-native UX:** Built for chaos. Driven by memes.
